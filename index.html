Walking call test script

  • Start walking in steady wind.
  • Say: "Can you hear me clearly?"
  • Answer: "Clear as day."
  • Read a 10-second paragraph (provided below).

Read-aloud paragraph

I'm heading down the street and the wind is picking up. I can still hear you clearly, and I'm speaking at a normal volume. Let's keep the call going.

Pass criteria

  • No repeats requested.
  • Voice stays consistent while moving.
  • Wind doesn't dominate the mic.
